How to Use

Instructions:

1. Enter any ingredient(s) you would like to be in the recipe in the "Include" field and press the green + button. Multiple ingredients can be included. (Types of dishes can also be entered. e.g. "soup")

2. Enter any ingredient(s) you would like to be excluded from the recipe in the "Exclude" field and press the green + button. (Types of dishes cannot be excluded.)

3. The Manage Ingredients button can be used to keep track of what you are currently searching for."

4. Under Advanced Options select any preferences you may have for the recipes you would like to see and press "OK" to confirm your selection.

5. Press Search to see the results of the criteria you have entered.
